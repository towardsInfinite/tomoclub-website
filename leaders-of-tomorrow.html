This book started as a question nobody could answer alone.

In early 2025, we asked school leaders across the country: What do you wish you had known before leading a future-ready school?

The answers were remarkable. Honest. Specific. Hard-earned. None of them had read a book that quite prepared them for this moment. Because that book did not exist yet. So we built it together.

Questions already answered

Is this really free?
Yes. Free during launch week (May 1–7, 2026). Enter your email and it arrives in your inbox. After launch week, available on Amazon at $24.99.
Who actually wrote this?
Fifteen practicing and former K–12 school leaders from 12 US states. Every chapter is written in the author's own voice. No ghostwriting.
How long is it?
Fifteen chapters across five thematic sections. Designed to read cover to cover in a weekend, or chapter by chapter as themes become relevant to your work.
Is this a TomoClub sales pitch?
No. The authors wrote independently about their own leadership experiences. TomoClub curated the project and is mentioned in the About section at the back.
Can I share this with my team?
Please do. The ebook is free to share, forward, and distribute. If you are a district leader, share it with your principals. The conversations it starts are the point.
